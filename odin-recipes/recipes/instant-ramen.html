Instant Ramen!

Description

This is some hot and fresh instant ramen!

You can make it anywhere!

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of Maruchan instant ramen noodles
  • 1 packet of ramen broth powder (from noodles package)
  • Optional ingredients - take your pick!
    • A soy-marinated boiled egg
    • Corn (frozen or canned)
    • Bok choy or lettuce
    • Kimchi
    • Enoki mushrooms
    • And anything else you can think of!

Steps

  1. Boil water and add ramen noodles. Cook noodles for 4 minutes.
  2. Add ramen broth powder and stir.
  3. Add optional ingredients.
  4. Serve while hot with chopsticks and a slurping spoon! :)
