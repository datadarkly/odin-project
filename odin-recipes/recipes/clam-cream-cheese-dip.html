Clam & Cream Cheese Dip

Description

This is some delicious clam and cream cheese dip!

It's very easy to make and requires few ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 1 can of clams
  • 1 stick of cream cheese
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Remove cream cheese from wrapping and place in a medium size bowl
  2. Mash cream cheese with a fork until formed to a liquid consistency
  3. Open clams and drain of liquid. Add clams to cream cheese.
  4. Mix dip together and refrigerate
