Golden Curry

Description

This is some delicious golden curry!

It takes a little work but it's worth it!

Ingredients

  • 1 Box of Golden Curry
  • One protein of your choice (try stew beef!)
  • Diced potatoes - I like using baby Dutch yellows
  • Optional ingredients - take your pick!
  • One bag of mixed vegetables - frozen is fine!

Steps

  1. Brown or cook protein entirely within a saucepan.
  2. Add mixed vegetables and diced potatoes. Saute lightly.
  3. Add 4 cups of water and bring to simmer. Cover and cook for 30 minutes.
  4. Add curry spice blocks and mix into pot. Serve over rice or noodles.
